Change of Course

TECH ED allows you to change your course to an alternative course only once using the following guidelines:

  • TECH ED is notified in writing at least 1 week before the commencement of the original course.
  • The alternative course has seats available and will be finished within 1 month of the original course finish date.
  • If switching to a course of a higher value, students are required to pay the amount difference.
  • A processing fee of Rs. 500/- needs to be paid within 3 days after TECH ED accepts change of course request.

Course change is allowed only once during the program.

Process for Change of Course

  1. Contact ACMEGRADE at tech.ed.sols@gmail.com with your course change request.
  2. Once approved, fill up the “Course Change Form” and submit it within 3 business days.
  3. Pay the processing fees + difference in course costs (if applicable) and submit the receipt to tech.ed.sols@gmail.com.

In 5-7 business days, you will get a confirmation for the course change request.

Reschedule

Rescheduling by TECH ED SOLUTIONS LLP are governed by the following guidelines:

  • If a course doesn’t begin within 30 business days of published commencement date, a student is entitled to either a full refund or can choose to join a different batch/course.
  • Reschedule request has to be made at least 10 business days before the commencement of the course.
  • A rescheduling fee of Rs. 500/- must be paid within 5 business days from the date of approval.

Cancellation

TECH ED reserves the right to cancel any session or change the location of the session for reasons such as Instructor unavailability, insufficient participants, or force majeure events.

Refund Process

For a refund request, the User may submit a request on the Company’s website. The Refund request shall be entertained and processed if the Company has failed to provide the course within 30 days of course starting date.

How Your Refund is Returned:

  • If your payment came from your card, it will be refunded to that card. It may take up to 30 days to complete a refund.
  • If your payment came from your bank account, it will be refunded to that bank account.
  • No cash refunds are made under any circumstances.

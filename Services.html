INTENSHIP PROCESS

Internship Process
A comprehensive 2-month program (45 days of expert-led training + 15 days of live project work), conducted completely online to suit your schedule.
Access to our Learning Management System (LMS) allows you to take the training at your own pace. Sessions are accessible anytime, letting you balance both college and the internship.
Learn from mentors with over 7 years of experience, guiding you through both fundamental and advanced concepts to build a solid skill set.

OUR SERVICE

HANDS-ON PROJECTS
Work on two live projects—a minor project individually and a major project with a team—providing you with real-world industry experience and collaborative skills.
CERTIFICATIONS
Earn 4 AICTE-approved certificates: course completion, internship completion, project completion, and an Outstanding Performer Certificate. Plus, a Letter of Recommendation from your mentor.
FUTURE OPPORTUNITIES
Exceptional performance during the program could lead to a 6-month paid internship with a stipend of ₹25,000 to ₹30,000, giving you a significant career advantage post-graduation. Internship programs that have a positive impact on the interns and the local community.
